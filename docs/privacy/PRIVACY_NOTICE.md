## 3. Personal Data We Collect

### 3.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information from employees:

- **Identity Information:**

  - Full name (first name, middle initial, last name)
  - Employee ID
  - Date of birth
  - Gender
  - Profile photograph

- **Contact Information:**

  - Residential address
  - Email address (if applicable)
  - Contact number (if applicable)

- **Employment Information:**

  - Hire date
  - Position/Job title
  - Job level
  - Assigned hotel/location
  - Monthly rate and per-day rate
  - Employment status (active/inactive)
  - Eligible for overtime status

- **Government Identification Numbers:**

  - Tax Identification Number (TIN)
  - Social Security System (SSS) number
  - Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth) number
  - Home Development Mutual Fund (Pag-IBIG) number

- **Benefits Information:**
  - Health Maintenance Organization (HMO) provider
  - Leave credits (SIL, maternity, paternity)
  - Offset hours

### 3.2 Sensitive Personal Information

We also process **sensitive personal information** as defined under Section 3(g) of the DPA:

- **Financial Information:**

  - Salary and compensation details
  - Payroll records
  - Deductions (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG contributions, loans, taxes)
  - Payslip information
  - Allowances and adjustments

- **Time and Attendance Data:**

  - Clock in/out records
  - GPS location data (for time clock verification)
  - Daily attendance records
  - Regular hours, overtime hours, night differential hours
  - Weekly attendance summaries

- **Leave and Time-Off Records:**

  - Leave requests (vacation, sick, emergency, etc.)
  - Leave approval records
  - Failure-to-log requests
  - Overtime requests and approvals
  - Supporting documents submitted with leave requests

- **Schedule Information:**
  - Work schedules
  - Shift assignments

### 3.3 System Usage Data

- Login credentials (employee ID and password)
- System access logs
- User activity logs
- IP addresses and device information

## 4. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your personal data for the following legitimate purposes:

### 4.1 Primary Purposes

1. **Employee Management:**

   - Maintaining employee records and profiles
   - Processing employment applications and onboarding
   - Managing employee assignments and schedules

2. **Payroll Processing:**

   - Calculating salaries, wages, and compensation
   - Processing government contributions (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG)
   - Computing deductions and taxes
   - Generating payslips
   - Processing allowances and adjustments

3. **Time and Attendance Management:**

   - Recording and tracking work hours
   - Verifying attendance through GPS location data
   - Calculating regular hours, overtime, and night differential
   - Managing work schedules

4. **Leave Management:**

   - Processing leave requests
   - Tracking leave credits and balances
   - Managing leave approvals
   - Processing failure-to-log requests

5. **Compliance and Legal Obligations:**

   - Complying with Philippine labor laws
   - Complying with tax regulations
   - Complying with social security requirements
   - Maintaining records as required by law

6. **System Security and Access Control:**
   - Authenticating user access
   - Monitoring system usage
   - Preventing unauthorized access
   - Maintaining system security

### 4.2 Secondary Purposes (with consent)

- Generating reports and analytics for business operations
- Improving HR processes and services
- Training and development purposes

## 8. Data Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data:

1. **Technical Security:**

   - Encryption of data in transit and at rest
   - Secure authentication mechanisms
   - Role-based access control (RBAC)
   - Row-level security (RLS) policies
   - Regular security updates and patches
   - Secure password policies

2. **Organizational Security:**

   - Confidentiality agreements
   - Regular security audits

3. **Physical Security:**
   - Secure data centers (managed by cloud providers)
   - Access controls to physical facilities

## 9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

- **Active Employee Records:** Retained during employment and for a period after termination as required by law
- **Payroll Records:** Retained for at least 3 years as required by the Bureau of Internal Revenue
- **Time and Attendance Records:** Retained for at least 3 years as required by labor laws
- **Leave Records:** Retained for at least 3 years
- **System Logs:** Retained for security and audit purposes

After the retention period, personal data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

## 10. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the following rights:

1. **Right to be Informed** - You have the right to be informed about the collection and processing of your personal data
2. **Right to Access** - You have the right to access and obtain a copy of your personal data
3. **Right to Object** - You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data
4. **Right to Erasure or Blocking** - You have the right to suspend, withdraw, or order the blocking, removal, or destruction of your personal data
5. **Right to Damages** - You have the right to be indemnified for any damages sustained due to inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, false, unlawfully obtained, or unauthorized use of your personal data
6. **Right to Data Portability** - You have the right to obtain your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format
7. **Right to File a Complaint** - You have the right to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC)
